Our Sweet Beginnings

From Home Kitchen to Your Heart

What started as a Sunday passion project in a tiny apartment kitchen has blossomed into CupcakeWin. Disha Sharma began baking with a simple mission: to create cupcakes that didn't just look good, but tasted like a warm hug.

After perfecting family recipes and experimenting with wild flavor combinations, the demand grew from friends and family to the entire neighborhood. Today, we still bake with that same love and attention to detail, ensuring every swirl of frosting is perfect.
